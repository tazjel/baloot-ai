# Privacy Policy — بلوت AI

**Effective Date**: February 2026
**Developer**: Baloot AI Team

## Overview
بلوت AI (Baloot AI) is a card game application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Data We Collect
### Stored Locally Only (on your device)
- **Player Name**: Optional nickname you choose for display
- **Game Statistics**: Games played, wins, losses, win streaks
- **Settings**: Difficulty level, theme preference, sound settings

### We Do NOT Collect
- No personal identification information (email, phone, address)
- No location data
- No usage analytics or tracking
- No advertising identifiers
- No payment information
- No social media accounts

## Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences (Android/iOS secure storage). No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

## Online Multiplayer
When using the optional online multiplayer feature:
- Only your chosen display name is shared with other players in the same game room
- No persistent accounts are created
- Game room data is temporary and deleted when the room closes

## Third-Party Services
This app does NOT use any third-party services that collect data:
- No Google Analytics
- No Firebase Analytics
- No Facebook SDK
- No advertising networks
- No crash reporting services

## Children's Privacy
This app does not knowingly collect any data from children. The app is suitable for all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

## Data Deletion
To delete all locally stored data:
1. Go to Settings → Clear Data within the app, OR
2. Uninstall the app from your device

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app update notes.
