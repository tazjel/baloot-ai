# Privacy Policy — Baloot AI (بلوت AI)

**Last updated:** February 2026

## Overview

Baloot AI ("the App") is a single-player card game developed for entertainment purposes. This privacy policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

## Data Collection

### Data We Collect
The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Player name | Display during gameplay | SharedPreferences (on-device) |
| Game statistics | Win/loss tracking, match history | SharedPreferences (on-device) |
| App preferences | Theme (dark/light), difficulty level, sound settings | SharedPreferences (on-device) |

### Data We Do NOT Collect
- **No personal information** (email, phone, address)
- **No account creation** required
- **No analytics or tracking** SDKs
- **No advertising** SDKs or ad networks
- **No crash reporting** to external services
- **No location data**
- **No camera or microphone** access
- **No contacts or calendar** access
- **No network data transmission** of user data

## Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Flutter's SharedPreferences API. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. The App functions entirely offline after installation.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all App data at any time by:
1. Clearing the App's data in your device settings, or
2. Uninstalling the App

All stored data will be permanently removed.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for all ages and contains no inappropriate content, in-app purchases, or advertising.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
